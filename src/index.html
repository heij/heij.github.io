MY PORTFOLIO

SPA Landing Page Vanilla.js anime.js

Yeah, this page right here! Built with vanilla.js (as both a fun challenge to myself and a way to hammer down the basics) and a sprinkle of anime.js to fuel some of the animations.

After deciding to put together a fun portfolio for myself, I picked sci-fi as the main theme, with sharp geometric shapes and a bit of the signature neon glows. I opted to design a single page that could be navigated solely through the scroll, making for a fast and easy read.

To put emphasis on the scroll idea, I wanted to add a few scroll-linked animations to the site. The final choice was a banner that acts as the header for each session, showing tiny pixel arts that change as the user navigates through the page.
